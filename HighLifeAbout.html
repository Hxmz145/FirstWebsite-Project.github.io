Our History

1965

In 1965,
three brothers embarked on a journey that would shape the course of their lives and establish a lasting legacy.
With the help of their father's will, the brothers bought a small property and formed a property management company.
Over the years, the company grew in size and reputation,
and its commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction became its hallmark.
With each passing year, the company expanded its portfolio,
taking on new properties and new challenges,
always with a steadfast commitment to providing the highest level of service to its clients.
Today, the company is recognized as a leader in the property management industry,
with a reputation for excellence that has endured for decades.
Despite the many changes that have taken place since its founding,
the company remains true to the values and principles that guided its founders,
and it continues to be driven by a passion for excellence and a dedication to the satisfaction of its clients.
